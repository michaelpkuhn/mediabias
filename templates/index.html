While the Liberal-Conservative Spectrum is familiar to most, the Y-Axis "Inter-Party Differences Spectrum" is not. This spectrum aims to scale the differences between members of the same party.

On the Democratic-Side, the higher side of the Y-Axis is populated by the Suburban Swing State Democrats like Elisa Slotkin and Lauren Underwood. The lower side consists of the Leftist side of the party like Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez and Rashida Tlaib.

On the Republican-Side, the lower side tends to be more Libertarian featuring Justin Amash (pre-independence) and Thomas Massie. On the higher side... Mitt Romney and Martha Roby.
